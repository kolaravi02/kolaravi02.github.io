  • Stoch3 quadruped

    Stoch lab, RBCCPS

    Abstract: Stoch3 has been our platform for testing controllers for over a year. We have implemented linear policy based force-control and convex optimization for a rigid body motion.

  • Dridh Biped

  • Elephant and Rabbit Robot(Ring tossing) - ABU ROBOCON 2023

    Gujarat Technological University

    Abstract: Follwing the Gameplay of ABU Robocon 2023, we prepared two robots which complete the gameplay of tossing rings onto ploes throughout the arena. Both robots were equipped with a twin-roller mechanism which allowed them to toss the rings with a pitch.

  • R1(Ball throwing) and R2(Lagori piling and ball picking) Robot - ABU ROBOCON 2022

    Gujarat Technological University

    Abstract: Follwing the Gameplay of ABU Robocon 2022, given by IIT Delhi, we prepared two robots which complete the game of lagori. Robot R2 was used for efficiently piling lagori discs and picking 6 balls at one run with a high-level controlled holonomic drive base. Robot R1 was used for displacing balls mounted on different poles at a hieght throught the game arena.
