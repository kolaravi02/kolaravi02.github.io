Ravi Kola

I am currently a Graduate Research Assistant at the Robert Bosch Centre for Cyber-Physical Systems at the Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru.

I develop robust control architectures for legged robotic platforms such as the Biped Robot Dridh and Quadruped Robot Stoch3. Additionally, I actively work on the electronics and software development for these robots. Check out our work at Stochlab. Prior to this, I was the Management head and member at GTU Robotics Club working for ABU Robocon 2023 and 2022.
